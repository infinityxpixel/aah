Privacy Policy for Blanked Last Updated: March 26, 2026 This Privacy Policy describes how Blanked ("the App") handles your information. 1. Information Collection and Use Blanked is designed to be a local-first experience. Personal Data: We do not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information (such as your name, email address, or phone number). Game Data: Any names entered during the "New Session" screen are stored only in the device's temporary memory (RAM) and are deleted when the app is closed. Usage Data: We do not track your location, device ID, or usage habits. 2. Third-Party Services The App is built using the Capacitor framework. While the app itself does not collect data, it may load resources (like fonts or scripts) from the following third-party Content Delivery Networks (CDNs): Google Fonts (for the Inter font) Tailwind CSS Unpkg.com (for React and Babel) These services may see your IP address when requesting these files, but they do not have access to your game data. 3. Permissions The App does not require access to your camera, microphone, contacts, or storage. 4. Children’s Privacy Since we do not collect any personal information, the App is safe for users of all ages. However, the content of the card game (humor) is intended for a mature audience. 5. Changes to This Policy We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. 6.
